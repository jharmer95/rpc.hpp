◆ free_buffer()

template<typename T >
void rpc::details::free_buffer ( const arg_buffer &  buffer)

Frees a dynamically allocated arg_buffer.

Due to the way arg_buffers work, they must be constructed using a 'placement new' and therefore do not have any helpful RAII method of being cleaned up. This function therefore, must be called to ensure their memory does not leak
